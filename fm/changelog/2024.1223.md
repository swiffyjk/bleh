# Navigation
- __Site-wide navigation has been modernised__
    - All tabs both at the top of the page and in subpages now resemble buttons and are much friendlier to click. A ringed outline is displayed around the currently focused tab.
- __The bleh settings now uses a row of tabs__
    - Finally matching the native last.fm settings
    - A lot of tabs with similar goals have been merged to make way for this change too, more steps to a less confusing settings.
- __The site-wide search bar is now easily accessible__
    - Rather than being hidden behind a broken button with an aging design it's now all front and centre!!
- __The current season along with a counter replaces the bleh settings button__
    - Hover over to see you can now get a live counter running when on the 'Seasonal' tab in settings :3
