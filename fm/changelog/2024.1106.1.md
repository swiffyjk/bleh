Following (kinda) coincidentally with Last.fm's debut of album image saving, bleh now features a fully redesigned gallery page.

![Redesigned gallery image](https://katelyynn.github.io/bleh/fm/changelog/img/bleh3-gallery.png)

### A walkthrough

Gallery pages have been restructured to place primary focus on the image in question, now stretching across both columns. To further help this, a new 'Expand' button is located below to view the image in it's full resolution. Along with this, a vote tally is now displayed taking the positive votes minus the negative votes to give you the current number used for ordering.
