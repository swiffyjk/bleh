# Wikis
- __Wiki pages have been redesigned__
    - This is less of a grand redesign compared to the recent gallery changes but still a massive improvement for those wiki editing (like myself)

![Wiki overview page](https://katelyynn.github.io/bleh/fm/changelog/img/wiki.png)

Wiki overviews now have the two common action buttons located for easy access in the sidebar. The most recent wiki author is now also inline with the title.

![Wiki edit page](https://katelyynn.github.io/bleh/fm/changelog/img/wiki-edit.png)

Wiki editing has received the biggest improvement in this update. The previously available wiki syntax guide is now accompanied with a selection of 6 symbol presets that can be used for writing wikis. These includes two stylised quotation marks, apostrophes, and two variations of the dash. Style rules have also been re-located into the sidebar to reduce the scrolling needed to save.

![Wiki history page](https://katelyynn.github.io/bleh/fm/changelog/img/wiki-history.png)

The wiki history has now been updated to fit the overall aesthetic of today's bleh, including longer summaries depending on username width. These items now also display custom user badges and highlight staff members.
